about me

Welcome to Cream.py, your number one source of high quality computer parts on affordable price. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of our product, with a focus on quality, and affordability.
Founded in 3/3/2021 by Jhon Keneth Ryan B. Namias on ICT 2, Cream.py has come a long way from its beginnings in a Young Achievers School of Caloocan. When Namias first started out, his passion for to create an shopping website which are affordable and selling high quality products at the same time drove him to create an website for Performance Task 1 & 2 in Computer Programming and Computer System Servicing, and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers all over YASC, and are thrilled to be a part of the YASC Programming Community.
We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Sincerely,
Jhon Keneth Ryan B. Namias (Founder)
